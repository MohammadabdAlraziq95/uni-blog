About Us

Najah’s Book is a website that belongs to An-Najah National University with all its departments in Nablus,Palestine. It seeks to facilitate and provide everything that the students might need through their connection to the university. This website consolidates and tracks all the resources need for the students, which makes it easier for then to find answers to their questions in the most effective way. In addition, students will be able to speak with the professors and other staff in the university, and they’ll be able to find the halls, classes an places of the university by using the map that is provided in this website, which would be especially really helpful and useful for new students. Moreover, it can also help the students to determine their educational plan that is in line with their majors.
